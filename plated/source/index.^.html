current things
Oct 2025 - Plug It Up [Plug It Up](https://bradfordsoundwomen.wordpress.com/plug-it-up/) is a DIY sound art festival combining workshops and performances led by female & marginalised gender artists who are working with sound in innovative ways. Really chuffed to have been a part of this ambitious event, the first of its kind in Bradford and by such a tiny team. July 2025 - Imagine Zine Space We, Loosely Bound zine collective, took over Trapezium Arts space for two days to make zines together during International Zine Month, thanks to some funding by Creative Lives. We feel lucky to have had this opportunity and think all spaces should do this for their community!
